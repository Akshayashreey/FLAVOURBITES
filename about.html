About Us

Achievements

Namma Veetu Sapadu takes pride in its numerous accolades, including the prestigious Best South Indian Restaurant title at the Foodie's Choice Awards for three consecutive years. These recognitions underscore our commitment to excellence and our relentless pursuit of culinary perfection.

Our restaurant has garnered rave reviews from satisfied customers. "The food here is incredible," says Samantha. "I've never tasted South Indian cuisine this authentic outside of home," adds Rahul. We are grateful for the continued support and feedback from our patrons.
